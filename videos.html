Personally for me one of the scariest parts of building a pc is making sure that you install the cpu proporly. The videos above will go over how to do such a task but take a quick look at this video below of a cpu install. Notice those tiny pins at the bottom? They are very fragile and if you bend or break any one of those pins your looking at replacing the whole CPU which in some cases is the most expensive part in the entire pc build so be very careful I cant stress this enough!
